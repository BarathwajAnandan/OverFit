Community Rules & Karma
Karma: +1 per upload. Upvotes on your uploads increase your karma. Flagged, faulty, or plagiarized uploads reduce karma and may lead to bans.
Plagiarism: Automated checks are in place. If plagiarism is detected, the content is removed and the account may be suspended.
Votes & Flags: Anyone can upvote/downvote. Flags send content to trusted reviewers for rapid review.
Trusted Reviewers: Elevated users who can resolve flags, restore content, and apply penalties when necessary.
Conduct: Share with consent, redact sensitive information, and respect licensing of third-party content.
Appeals: If you believe a moderation action was in error, submit an appeal for re-evaluation.
