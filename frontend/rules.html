Community Rules & Points
Seed: Earned by contributing — uploading context, answering unsolved questions, improving solutions. Each accepted contribution typically gives +1 Seed.
Leech: Spent when requesting premium help. Each premium request consumes 1 Leech.
Life: Your available credit: Life = Seed − Leech. You need Life ≥ 1 to request premium help.
Voting: Upvotes surface quality. Repeated low-quality or plagiarized content may lead to penalties.
Conduct: Share with consent, redact sensitive information, and respect licensing of third-party content.
Appeals: If you believe a moderation action was in error, submit an appeal for re-evaluation.
